Who are we?

Lox Game Studio is a small group. This studio has opened at 2012 by LordOf Xen. And our workers are 36 people. We are just making mods on Source engine and making libraries. We are able to make Discord, Steam, Skype bots.

There are only 3 programmers in our studio. But most of the time, LordOf Xen is working alone. The others are just translators, idea helpers, intro makers and etc.
If you want to be a developer or a programmer in Lox Game Studio, contact with us.

What is Notarged 2?

Notarged 2 is a survival mod from us. We are still working on and we will try to publish it as fast as possible for us. Maps will have their own objectives and missions to do. And as we mentioned on our every mods, we making this mod for learning the engine.
